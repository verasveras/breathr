Breathr is a relaxation tool that helps you breathe. Studies show that breathwork can help you reduce stress, manage anxiety, and feel more focused. With Breathr, you simply inhale while the circle gets bigger and exhale as it gets smaller.

Breathr gives you the power to control the lengths of your inhales and exhales. For a starting point, we suggest setting your inhale-to-exhale ratio to 1-to-2 for 10 breaths, but play around with different configurations and see what works for you!
